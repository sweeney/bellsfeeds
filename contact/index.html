Directions:

From Knutsford:
South on the A50 for approx 2 miles. Turn right onto B5081, Middlewich Road.
After ¾ mile, turn right into Ullard Hall Lane.
Farm is at the very end of this lane after approx. 1 mile.
From Holmes Chapel:
North on the A50 from Holmes Chapel for approx 3 miles. Pass Radbrook Hall (Barclays) or the 'Whipping Sticks Pub' on right hand side.
After ½ mile, turn left onto B5081 Middlewich Road. Etc. as above.
From Middlewich:
Take B5081 Byley Lane for approx 4 miles to Lower Peover heading for Knutsford.
1½ miles beyond Lower Peover, turn sharp left into Ullard Hall Lane
Farm is at the very end of this lane after approx. 1 mile.
